Workout App

I developed an app that provides users with a convenient interface for training, enhanced with advanced features.

Workout Tab:

  • Integrated a single workout consisting of 8 exercises.
  • Added a timer to track the duration of each exercise.
  • After completing a workout, 1 rest day is automatically added with an option to cancel it, allowing users to continue training without waiting.
  • The cycle of workouts and rest days repeats endlessly, ensuring a continuous training process.

Charts and Results Tab:

  • This tab contains charts and user workout results.
  • Tracks the number of completed workouts for each body area or other parameters.

Profile Tab:

  • Users can input their details: name, age, physical parameters.
  • Includes the option to change the app's language.
  • Feature to share the app with friends.
  • Buttons for app rating and developer feedback.
  • Option to purchase a PRO version for full ad removal.

Additional Features:

  • The app includes built-in ads, which are removed after purchasing the PRO version.
  • Voice guidance for exercises: a narrator announces each movement during workouts.
  • Option to switch to dark mode for nighttime training.

This app allows users to personalize their workouts, track progress, and improve their results.
